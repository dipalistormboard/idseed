About the Tool

This seed identification tool contains Canadian regulated plant species in the Weed Seeds Order and plant species in the Pests regulated by Canada under the Canadian authority of Seed Act and Plant Protection Act respectively. Common Canadian weeds that are grading factors under Seed Regulations are also included in the tool. This is the most comprehensive seed identification reference available for Canadian regulated or common weed species with seed identification factsheets, family identification key, diagnostic seed image galleries, and botany terminologies for seed identification.

Features of the tool:

  • Seed id fact sheet contains multiple seed identification/diagnostic images or drawings, feature descriptions, similar species comparisons and information on potential growth habitats and contamination sources for each plant species.
  • Family feature and glossary aid seed identification and analyst training
  • The gallery of seed images and illustrations serve as a searchable database to facilitate seed identification and to memorize seed morphological features.

This tool is its first edition and updated versions will be available in the future to include more plant species and to enrich the tool.
