Theme Preview

Theme Description

This theme features a large preview image with four columns of preview images. Moving your mouse over a thumbnail puts that image into the preview space. It also includes clickable thumbnails that open into pop-up windows.

Usage

In Fact Sheet Fusion's Export settings:

  1. In Select Fact Sheet Template choose this theme file.
  2. In Select Destination for Fact Sheets choose a folder to place your published sheets in.
  3. Make sure Skip Media is not checked.
  4. Choose a size for your regular scale images. This theme works well with regular images between 350 and 550 pixels. Choose 350 pixels as a good starting point.
  5. Check Scale regular images and enter the pixel size you picked in step 4.
  6. Choose a size for your thumbnail images. This theme works well with thumbnail images between 80 and 250 pixels. Choose 150 pixels as a good starting point.
  7. Check Scale thumbnail images and enter the pixel size you picked in step 6.
  8. Check Create Glossary.
  9. Click Export.

After you export your fact sheets you must copy your original (full-sized) images into a folder called 'large' in the output directory you selected in step 2 above. The structure and naming of the folders and images inside the large folder must match those used by Fact Sheet Fusion. The images from this folder will be used in the pop-up images on your fact sheet.
