Fact Sheets

Goat's-rue (Galega officinalis) seeds

Goat's-rue (Galega officinalis) seed

Goat's-rue (Galega officinalis) seed, hilum view

Galega officinalis

Family

Fabaceae

Common Name

Goat's-rue

Regulation

  • Primary Noxious, Class 2 in the Canadian Weed Seeds Order (2016) under the Seeds Act.

Distribution

Canadian: Occurs in ON, QC (Darbyshire 2003, Brouillet et al. 2010+).

Worldwide: Native to northern Africa, Europe, Turkey and Pakistan (USDA-ARS 2016). Introduced in Canada, the United States, Argentina, Chile and New Zealand (CABI 2016).

Duration of Life Cycle

Perennial

Seed/Fruit Type

Seed

Seed Identification Features

Average Seed Size

  • Length: 3.5 - 4.2 mm

  • Width: 1.5 - 2.0 mm

  • Thickness: 1.0 - 1.5 mm

Seed Shape

  • Long oval with a side notch

Seed Surface Texture

  • Dull, slightly roughened

Seed Colour

  • Most seeds are a dull greenish-yellow, some are reddish-brown

Other Seed Features

  • Hilum is round and surrounded by a ring of raised tissue.
  • Groove evident between radicle and cotyledons.

Habitat and Crop Association

Old fields, pastures, rangelands, grasslands, fencelines, shores, riverbanks, roadsides and disturbed areas (Darbyshire 2003, CABI 2016). Reported as a weed in alfalfa (Whitson et al. 2000).

General Information

Goat's-rue has a history of use as a bee plant, forage and medicinal plant (USDA-ARS 2016). It was initially introduced into Utah in the 1890s as a forage plant, but was found to contain an alkaloid toxic to livestock (Tingey 1971).

This species has since infested moist, waterlogged and acidic soils in pastures and old fields in Utah, where it is proving difficult to eradicate (Lasseigne 2003).

Similar Species

Oriental goat's-rue (Galega orientalis)

Oriental goat's-rue seeds have a similar size, long oval shape and round hilum as goat's-rue

Oriental goat's-rue seeds are approximately 0.3 mm wider, have a pointed base, glossy surface and a shallow or no radicle groove. Goat's-rue seeds are dull with a round base and pronounced radicle groove.
